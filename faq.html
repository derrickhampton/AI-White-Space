What is a zero-width space?

A zero-width space (U+200B) is an invisible Unicode character used for formatting and line-breaking. It doesn’t display, but it can disrupt search indexing, copying, formatting, and SEO keyword matching. AI tools and copy/paste workflows often introduce hidden Unicode characters like zero-width joiners, non-breaking spaces (NBSP), and word joiners.

How does the Hidden Character Detector work?

The HiddenChars tool scans your pasted text in your browser and detects invisible Unicode whitespace such as:

  • Zero-width space (U+200B)
  • Zero-width joiner (U+200D)
  • Zero-width non-joiner (U+200C)
  • Non-breaking space (NBSP, U+00A0)
  • Word joiner (U+2060)

When hidden characters are detected, the tool highlights them and helps you remove invisible Unicode safely before downloading a clean, sanitized version for publishing, search, and reliable formatting.

Why do invisible characters matter for SEO?

Invisible whitespace can break keyword matching, prevent exact searches from working, and introduce subtle formatting issues that impact content quality. Removing hidden Unicode characters helps ensure clean copy, accurate indexing, and improved technical SEO.

How does the AI Humanizer tool work?

Humanize rewrites stiff or robotic AI-generated content using case-preserving transformations and context-aware, rule-based rewriting to improve flow and readability. It helps by:

  • Replacing robotic transition phrases
  • Reducing repetitive sentence structure
  • Applying natural contractions (optional)
  • Softening long run-on sentences (optional)

The goal is natural, human-sounding writing while preserving meaning and tone.

Does HiddenChars store my text?

No. Text processing occurs in your browser. HiddenChars does not intentionally store or log pasted text from the detection or humanize tools.

When should I use the Hidden Character Tool?

Use it when:

  • Your text breaks unexpectedly when pasted
  • Search/replace isn’t working correctly
  • Keywords don’t match due to hidden whitespace
  • AI-generated content contains invisible Unicode characters
