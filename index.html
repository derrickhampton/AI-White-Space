What is HiddenChars?

HiddenChars is a powerful zero-width space detector and invisible Unicode character remover designed to scan, highlight, and clean hidden whitespace from AI-generated and copied text. It detects problematic characters such as zero-width spaces (U+200B), zero-width joiners, zero-width non-joiners, non-breaking spaces (NBSP), word joiners, and other invisible Unicode formatting marks that can disrupt SEO, formatting, indexing, and search accuracy. Paste your content into our hidden character scanner and instantly analyze your text for invisible characters. If hidden whitespace is found, a detailed pop-up identifies exactly which Unicode characters are present and where they appear. We highlight them in context, allowing you to remove invisible characters and download a clean, sanitized version for reliable copying, publishing, code formatting, and search engine optimization.
