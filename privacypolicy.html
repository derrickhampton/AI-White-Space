Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how HiddenChars (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit or use our website and tools.

Effective date: 2026-02-17

Quick summary: We design HiddenChars so your pasted text is processed in your browser whenever possible. We use analytics to understand site usage. If you email us, we’ll receive what you send.

1) Information we collect

We may collect information in the following categories:

  • Information you provide: If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.
  • Usage information: We may collect information about how the site is used (e.g., pages visited, time on page, referring URLs) through analytics tools.
  • Device and log information: Your browser may automatically send information such as IP address, device identifiers, browser type, and operating system.

2) Text you paste into our tools

HiddenChars tools are designed to process text in your browser whenever possible. We do not intentionally collect or store the text you paste into the tool unless a specific feature explicitly states otherwise.

Tip: Avoid pasting sensitive personal information into any online tool.

3) How we use information

We may use information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the HiddenChars website and tools
  • Respond to your requests and support inquiries
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends, performance, and security
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and misuse

4) Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies (including Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site and to improve performance and user experience. Analytics may collect information such as pages viewed, time spent, approximate location, and device/browser information.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5) Sharing and disclosure

We may share information:

  • With service providers who help us operate the site (e.g., hosting, analytics) and who are required to protect the information.
  • For legal reasons if we believe disclosure is required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • In a business transfer if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (we’ll provide notice if required).

We do not sell your personal information.

6) Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Support emails may be retained to help resolve issues and improve service.

7) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8) Your choices

  • Cookies: Manage cookies in your browser settings.
  • Email: You can contact us to request updates or deletion of email communications you have sent (where feasible).
  • Analytics opt-out: You may be able to use browser add-ons or settings to limit analytics tracking.

9) Children’s privacy

HiddenChars is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10) International visitors

If you access HiddenChars from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate. By using the site, you consent to this processing where permitted by law.
